# User Types & Roles (Non‑Technical)

## Administrator
- **What they can see:** Full system view across students, academics, finance, and system activity.
- **What they can do:** Create and manage user accounts, set system options, review activity, and manage academic and finance data.
- **What decisions they can make:** Policy and operational decisions, access approvals, and system‑wide changes.

## Registrar
- **What they can see:** Student records, enrollment details, and results.
- **What they can do:** Manage enrollments, approve or publish results, and issue transcripts.
- **What decisions they can make:** Enrollment approvals, result approvals, and official record issuance.

## Faculty
- **What they can see:** Their assigned classes, enrolled students, attendance, and assessment records.
- **What they can do:** Mark attendance, create assessments, and enter results for their classes.
- **What decisions they can make:** Academic performance evaluations for their assigned students.

## Exam Cell
- **What they can see:** Results prepared by faculty and related academic records.
- **What they can do:** Publish and finalize results.
- **What decisions they can make:** Official release and locking of exam results.

## Finance
- **What they can see:** Fee plans, student fee status, and payment records.
- **What they can do:** Create fee plans, issue payment vouchers, record and verify payments, and run finance reports.
- **What decisions they can make:** Financial clearance and fee‑related approvals.

## Coordinator
- **What they can see:** Student lists and academic setup data.
- **What they can do:** Import student data and support bulk operations.
- **What decisions they can make:** Data readiness for new intakes and bulk updates.

## Student
- **What they can see:** Their own academic records, results, attendance, and fee information.
- **What they can do:** View records and request official documents.
- **What decisions they can make:** Personal academic planning based on results and fee status.
