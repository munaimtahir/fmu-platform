# SIMS Overview for FMU Leadership

## Slide 1 — Title
- Student Information Management System (SIMS)
- Leadership briefing for FMU

## Slide 2 — Why This Matters
- Replaces paper files and manual tracking
- Reduces delays and errors
- Improves compliance and transparency

## Slide 3 — Who Uses the System
- Administrators, registrars, faculty, exam cell, finance, coordinators, students

## Slide 4 — What Administrators Can Do
- Manage users and access
- Set system rules and options
- Review activity and key metrics

## Slide 5 — Core Academic Workflows
- Admissions to enrollment
- Attendance and assessments
- Results and transcripts

## Slide 6 — Finance & Fees
- Fee plans and vouchers
- Payments and reports
- Student fee statements

## Slide 7 — Dashboards & Oversight
- System‑level counts
- Recent activity
- Attendance summary

## Slide 8 — Current Readiness
- Core academic and finance functions are in place
- Administration tools are available

## Slide 9 — Key Gaps to Address
- Password change and reset process
- Longer‑term reporting windows
- Settings history and bulk user updates

## Slide 10 — Recommended Next Steps
- Run a small pilot
- Provide training
- Finalize policy rules and reporting needs
