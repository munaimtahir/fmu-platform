# Features Explained Like a Brochure

## Student Records & Admissions
- **What it does:** Keeps a complete student profile from admission through graduation, including programs, batches, and departments.
- **Why it is useful:** Replaces paper files with a single source of truth for student data.
- **Who uses it:** Administrators, registrars, coordinators, and faculty.

## Academic Structure & Scheduling
- **What it does:** Organizes programs, courses, sections, academic periods, and timetables.
- **Why it is useful:** Ensures classes and academic terms are defined clearly before teaching begins.
- **Who uses it:** Administrators, registrars, and faculty.

## Enrollment Management
- **What it does:** Enrolls students into sections and supports bulk enrollment for large intakes.
- **Why it is useful:** Reduces manual errors and speeds up start‑of‑term setup.
- **Who uses it:** Administrators, registrars, and coordinators.

## Attendance Management
- **What it does:** Records attendance for classes and provides summary reports, including eligibility checks.
- **Why it is useful:** Makes attendance compliance easy to monitor and enforce.
- **Who uses it:** Faculty and administrators.

## Assessments, Gradebook, and Results
- **What it does:** Creates assessments, records scores, and publishes official results.
- **Why it is useful:** Gives a clear, controlled path from grading to final result release.
- **Who uses it:** Faculty, exam cell, registrars, and students.

## Finance & Fees
- **What it does:** Sets fee plans, issues payment vouchers, records payments, and provides finance reports.
- **Why it is useful:** Helps track student dues and supports financial decision‑making.
- **Who uses it:** Finance staff, administrators, and students (viewing their own fees).

## Administration & Governance
- **What it does:** Manages user accounts, roles, and system settings; records a history of key actions.
- **Why it is useful:** Supports access control and accountability across the institution.
- **Who uses it:** Administrators.

## Dashboards & Monitoring
- **What it does:** Shows high‑level statistics and recent activity for leadership oversight.
- **Why it is useful:** Provides quick visibility into overall system health and activity.
- **Who uses it:** Administrators and leadership.
