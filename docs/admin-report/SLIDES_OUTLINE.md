## Slide Content Guidelines

### Visual Design
- **Keep It Simple**: Clean, uncluttered slides
- **Use Consistent Formatting**: Same fonts, colors, layouts
- **Include Visuals**: Charts, diagrams, screenshots when possible
- **Limit Text**: Bullet points, not paragraphs
- **Use White Space**: Don't overcrowd slides

### Content Style
- **Plain Language**: Avoid technical jargon
- **Focus on Value**: Benefits over features
- **Be Specific**: Use numbers and examples
- **Be Honest**: Acknowledge challenges
- **Be Confident**: System is ready

### Presentation Tips
- **Practice**: Know your material
- **Time Management**: Respect time limits
- **Engage Audience**: Ask for questions
- **Have Backup**: Bring detailed documents
- **Stay Positive**: Emphasize readiness

## Alternative Presentations

### Quick Executive Briefing (5-7 minutes)
**Slides**: 1, 2, 3, 6, 11, 15, 18
**Focus**: High-level overview and recommendation

### Detailed Technical Review (30-40 minutes)
**Slides**: All slides, plus additional technical detail slides
**Focus**: Comprehensive review with Q&A

### Department-Specific Presentation
**Customize**: Focus on slides relevant to specific department
**Examples**:
- **For Faculty**: Slides 1, 2, 4, 5, 9 (Faculty section), 10, 15
- **For Finance**: Slides 1, 2, 4, 8 (Finance section), 9 (Finance), 10, 15
- **For Administration**: Slides 1-20 (full presentation)

## Questions to Prepare For

**Common Questions**:

1. **"How much does this cost?"**
   - Emphasize investment in training, not just technology
   - Show cost savings over time

2. **"How long will implementation take?"**
   - 3-4 months from pilot to full rollout
   - Explain phased approach

3. **"What if staff resist?"**
   - Comprehensive training
   - Gradual rollout
   - Strong support

4. **"Is it really ready?"**
   - Yes, core functionality complete
   - System deployed and tested
   - Ready for pilot

5. **"What are the risks?"**
   - Low risk, manageable challenges
   - Pilot approach minimizes risk
   - Risks primarily adoption-related
