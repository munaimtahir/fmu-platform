## How to Use This Document

This guide explains each screen in simple language:

**No technical jargon** - just plain English explanations.

## üîê Entry Point

### Login Screen
**Screenshot**: `login.png`

**Purpose**: This is where everyone starts. It's the front door to the system.

**Who Uses It**: Everyone - students, faculty, administrators

**What You Do Here**:
1. Type your email address
2. Type your password
3. Click "Login"

**What Happens Next**: The system recognizes who you are and shows you your personal dashboard.

**For Administrators**:
- You can give users their login credentials
- Passwords can be reset if forgotten
- System tracks who logs in (for security)

## üìö Academic Structure Setup

### Programs Management
**Screenshot**: `academics_programs.png`

**Purpose**: Define what degrees you offer.

**Who Uses It**: Registrar, academic coordinators

**What It Does**: Lists all degree programs (MBBS, BDS, Nursing, etc.)

**What You Can Do**:
- Add a new program (like "MBBS")
- Edit program details (duration, credits required)
- Remove outdated programs

**When You Use It**: Once during setup, rarely after that

**Example Use**: "We're starting a new BDS program next year, let me add it here."

---

### Batches Management
**Screenshot**: `academics_batches.png`

**Purpose**: Organize students by their year of entry.

**Who Uses It**: Registrar, coordinators

**What It Does**: Creates groups like "Class of 2024", "Class of 2025"

**What You Can Do**:

- View which students are in each batch

**When You Use It**: At the start of each academic year

**Example Use**: "Students entering in 2026 will be 'Batch 2026'."

**Think of It As**: Like grade levels in school, but for university.

## Summary for Administrators

### Easy to Use?
**Yes** - Most screens are straightforward:
- Clear labels
- Obvious buttons
- Logical flow

### Need Training?
**Minimal** - Most staff will understand with brief orientation:
- Login and navigate
- Their specific role's screens (1-2 hours training)
- Common tasks practice

### Who Needs What?

**Registrar Staff**: Academic management, students, enrollment  
**Finance Staff**: Finance module only  
**Faculty**: Attendance, gradebook, dashboards  
**Exam Cell**: Exams, results, assessments  
**IT Admin**: System administration  
**Students**: Dashboards, results, finance view

Most users will only use 5-10 screens regularly.
